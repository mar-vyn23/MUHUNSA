MAKERERE UNIVERSITY HUMAN NUTRITION STUDENTS' ASSOCIATION (MUHUNSA)

Full Disclosure

The Makerere University Human Nutrition Students' Association (MUHUNSA) is a student-led organization established to promote the welfare and academic pursuits of students studying human nutrition at Makerere University's School of Food Technology, Nutrition and Bioengineering. Our purpose is to achieve the objectives outlined in this Constitution.

Vision

Our vision is to cultivate a diverse and inclusive community of future nutrition leaders, equipped with academic, professional, and personal skills to drive positive change towards a healthier and more sustainable world through safe and nutritious food access.

Mission

To inspire and empower the next generation of nutrition leaders by providing a dynamic platform for academic, professional, and personal growth. We strive to create a diverse, inclusive, and collaborative community that fosters intellectual curiosity, innovative thinking, and practical skills in the field of human nutrition.

Objectives

  • Promote unity and professional development among Human Nutrition Students.
  • Effectively represent and advocate for the ideas and prospects of Human Nutrition Students.
  • Educate and serve the community by creating awareness, participating in research programs, and organizing academic and outreach activities.

Core Values

  1. Inclusivity: We value diversity and inclusiveness, creating a welcoming and supportive community for all human nutrition students.
  2. Excellence: We aim to promote academic excellence and encourage students to reach their full potential in the field of human nutrition.
  3. Responsibility: We take our role in the community and the world seriously, acting with integrity, accountability, and a commitment to making a positive impact.
  4. Collaboration: We believe in the power of working together and value collaboration and teamwork to achieve our goals.
  5. Innovation: We embrace new ideas, approaches, and technologies, encouraging students to think creatively in the pursuit of new knowledge and better solutions.
  6. Community: We believe in the importance of community, dedicated to serving the needs of human nutrition students and the wider community through education and outreach initiatives.
